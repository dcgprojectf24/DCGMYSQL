Our Project

The Database Consulting Group (DCG) is collaborating with Hamilton Library to revolutionize its data management for the Hawaiian and Pacific Collection.

By replacing outdated Excel-based workflows with a robust relational database, our project enhances accessibility, organization, and efficiency for both librarians and users.
